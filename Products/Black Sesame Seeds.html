About Our Black Sesame Seeds

MaxwellFoods presents premium Black Sesame Seeds, known for their deep black color and intense nutty aroma. These seeds are carefully selected and sorted to ensure size consistency and freedom from impurities.

Rich in essential minerals like calcium, iron, and magnesium, as well as powerful antioxidants, black sesame seeds are often regarded as a superfood. In Ayurveda and traditional Asian medicine, they are prized for their health-promoting properties.

Our black sesame seeds are perfect for adding visual contrast and texture to sushi, salads, and breads. They are also widely used in the preparation of desserts, black sesame paste, and oil extraction for medicinal purposes.
