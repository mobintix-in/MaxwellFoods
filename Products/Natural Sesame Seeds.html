About Our Natural Sesame Seeds

MaxwellFoods brings you premium Natural Sesame Seeds, carefully sourced and cleaned to remove impurities while retaining the seed's natural coat. These seeds are celebrated for their distinctive nutty flavor and high nutritional value.

Our natural seeds contain the full oil content of the original seed, making them excellent for oil extraction and crushing. They offer a crunchier texture compared to hulled seeds and a more robust flavor profile, making them a favorite for traditional breads, cookies, and savory dishes.

Sorted using advanced color sorting machinery, we guarantee a product that is free from dust, stones, and other foreign materials. Whether for direct consumption or industrial processing, our natural sesame seeds meet global export standards.
