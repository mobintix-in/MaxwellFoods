Our Story

MaxwellFoods was founded in 2012 by Chef Maxwell Thompson, who envisioned bringing restaurant-quality meals to busy families and professionals. Starting from a small commercial kitchen, we've grown into a premier prepared meal service and catering company serving the entire metropolitan area.

What began as a passion project—creating nutritious, delicious meals for friends and family—quickly evolved into a full-fledged business as word spread about our exceptional food quality and commitment to using only the freshest ingredients.

Today, we operate from our state-of-the-art 10,000 sq. ft. kitchen facility, where our team of certified chefs prepares hundreds of meals daily for individuals, families, and corporate clients.

Our Mission

To provide convenient, nutritious, and delicious meal solutions that support healthy lifestyles while promoting sustainable food practices and supporting local producers.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of premium prepared meals and catering services, recognized for exceptional quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction nationwide.

Our Journey

2012 - The Beginning

MaxwellFoods founded with a small kitchen and big dreams. Started with weekly meal delivery for 20 local families.

2015 - First Expansion

Moved to a larger facility and launched our corporate catering division. Served our first major corporate client with 500+ attendees.

2018 - Certification & Recognition

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification. Won "Best Prepared Meal Service" award from City Food Magazine for three consecutive years.

2023 - Present

Now serving 5,000+ weekly meals, operating from our new eco-friendly kitchen, and expanding our delivery radius to cover the entire metro area.
