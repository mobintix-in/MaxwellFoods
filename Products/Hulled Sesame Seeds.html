About Our Hulled Sesame Seeds

MaxwellFoods offers top-tier Hulled Sesame Seeds, processed using state-of-the-art mechanical hulling technology. This process removes the outer husk while preserving the seed's natural richness and flavor. The result is a uniformly white, clean, and nutritious seed.

Our hulled seeds are renowned for their purity (99.95%) and sweet, nutty flavor. They are an essential ingredient in the bakery industry for breads, buns, and cookies, as well as in the production of premium Tahini and Halva.

We ensure that our seeds are free from any chemical processing, maintaining their organic integrity. Rigorous quality control checks at every stage ensure that you receive only the best product, free from impurities and foreign matter.
